Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

RepWing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform at repwing.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Business name, email address, and contact information when you sign up
  • Customer phone numbers and email addresses entered by business owners for the purpose of sending review requests
  • Feedback and review responses submitted through our platform

We also collect usage data automatically, such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited, to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver SMS and email review request messages on behalf of business owners
  • Route customer feedback to the appropriate business owner
  • Recover missed calls by sending automated SMS notifications to callers
  • Improve and maintain our platform
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services

3. SMS Messaging & End-User Consent

RepWing is a B2B platform. Business owners ("Clients") use RepWing to send SMS messages to their own customers ("End-Users"). Clients are responsible for obtaining proper written or verbal consent from their End-Users before sending any messages through the RepWing platform.

All SMS messages sent through RepWing include opt-out instructions. End-Users may reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from messages. Message and data rates may apply.

We do not sell or share End-User phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Twilio — our SMS delivery provider, for the purpose of sending text messages
  • Service providers — who assist us in operating our platform (hosting, analytics, email delivery)
  • Legal authorities — if required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at james@repwing.com.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at james@repwing.com.

8. Children's Privacy

RepWing is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
